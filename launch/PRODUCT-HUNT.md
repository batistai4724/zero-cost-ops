## First Comment (Maker Story)

Hi PH 👋

I'm Ana, solo founder of NEYEN / The Spiral Within — a digital wellbeing platform built on Supabase, n8n, GitHub, and Cloudflare.

At some point I was paying ~$80/month for monitoring tools. Sentry, n8n Cloud, status page services. I was bootstrapped and the bill was bugging me. So I cut it all and spent two weekends building this instead.

**What zero-cost-ops actually is:**

A CTO cockpit PWA that shows traffic-light status for all your services, an incident log, and a next-actions list. Behind it, n8n workflows auto-ingest content from Slack/GitHub/wherever, Claude AI classifies it into structured metadata, and everything lands in Supabase.

Every morning I open it on my phone, tap Sync, read one line of text, and start my day. Takes 60 seconds.

**The $0/month claim is real:**
- n8n on Render free tier (with UptimeRobot keep-alive)
- Supabase free tier (500MB, 50K API calls/month)
- GitHub Pages (100GB bandwidth, forever free)
- Cloudflare DNS (genuinely free)
- Claude API: optional, ~$0.40/month at my volume

**Everything is open source.** The dashboard, the n8n workflow JSONs, the Supabase schema, step-by-step deployment guide. Fork and deploy in under 30 minutes.

Built for solo founders who want CTO-grade visibility without the SaaS bill.
